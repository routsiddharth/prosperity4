## Critical caveat: KO barrier is unknown

`AC_45_KO` (knock-out put) — the **knock-out barrier** is not stated in the challenge. Reverse-engineering from market mid (0.1625), the implied barrier is **between 35 and 40 (≈ 37)**. If the barrier is actually 45 (= strike), the option's fair value is ~0.003 and the market price is *massively* overpriced. If the barrier is 35, fair is ~0.25 and the market is *underpriced*.

This single product is a coin-flip swinging the optimal portfolio's expected PnL by ±$220k. **Confirm the barrier from the actual problem statement / dropdown UI before sending the trade.**
